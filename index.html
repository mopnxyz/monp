MOPN
MOPN is a Map Of Popular NFTs, with $MT as the economic driver. By simple rules of collaboration and competition, MOPN will help NFT collectors to gain more profits and attention.
Based on Ethereum, Fully On-chain Game, Fair launch, Open Source, No Admin.
How To Play
Users use any NFT they already own to place it on an unoccupied tiles on the map, and continue to gain $MT over time.
The 6 tiles around the place where the NFT is placed are territories. The territories of the same collection must be adjacent or overlapping, and the territories of different collection cannot overlap.
$MT can be used to auction bombs, which can remove the NFTs that have been placed on the map, making the tile available for new NFT to be placed.

Value

The internal value of MOPN
comes from the flexible strategies generated by multi-player games under simple rules, as well as the enjoyment derived from community collaboration and competition during the process.

The external value of MOPN
comes from the attention captured through the NFT community's changing territories, which translates into advertising and reputational value derived from occupying land.
